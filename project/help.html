Frequent question about hiking

What should I pack for a hike?

A well-packed backpack is essential, sufficient water and snacks, sun protection,first aid kit,etc.

How do I choose the best hiking boots or shoes?

Waterproof hiking boots for most conditions. Comfortable trail running shoes for easier hikes.

It is important to know the path?

Yes, especially if the path is not very clear, it is highly recomended to review the area and path.
